Histograph

There are many resources immediately available on the web, but it's very difficult to get a firm grasp on how we use them and even harder to discover new content that we find interesting. Current tools used to look at browsing habits do not do a great job of revealing patterns in browsing history. Content discovery sites like Reddit and StumbleUpon are community-based and give suggestions based on community (or moderator) opinions. Histograph solves both of these problems: we provide users with visual tools to help users get a firm grasp on and identify patterns in their browsing habits.
