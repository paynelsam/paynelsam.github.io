Structural Health Monitoring

I worked as a member of Professor Naveen Verema's team studying the use of thin-film electronics in the application of structural health monitoring. The creation of thin-film transistors allows thin-film sensors to be used as an effective, accurate, and cost-efficient means for tracking the integrity of large structures. I wrote software to interface with sensor arrays and to provide the necessary communication capabilities for the arrays to be formed into a larger network.
